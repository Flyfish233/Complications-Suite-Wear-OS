HOW TO SET UP COMPLICATION 1. long press watch center 2. tap customize 3. add complication - scroll down - choose one of the available complications SUPPORTED COMPLICATION TYPES • Date - SHORT_TEXT, LONG_TEXT • Week of Year - SHORT_TEXT, LONG_TEXT, RANGED_VALUE (0 -> 52) • Day of Year - SHORT_TEXT, LONG_TEXT, RANGED_VALUE (0 -> 365) • World Clock / UTC / 2 - SHORT_TEXT, LONG_TEXT • Seconds - SHORT_TEXT, LONG_TEXT • Pay (shortcut) - ICON, SMALL_IMAGE • Assistant (shortcut) - ICON, SMALL_IMAGE • Wear OS logo (shortcut) - ICON, SMALL_IMAGE • AWF logo (shortcut) - ICON, SMALL_IMAGE • Moon Phase (+visibility) - ICON, SMALL_IMAGE, SHORT_TEXT, RANGED_VALUE TAP ON COMPLICATION • World Clock / UTC - launches time zone setting activity • Seconds - launches "ADD ALARM" activity • Week of Year, Day of Year, Date - launches "CALENDAR" activity • Pay shortcut - launches G Pay • Assistant shortcut - launches G Assistant • Wear OS logo - launches "about watch" in settings • AWF logo - opens our watch face catalog • Moon phase - launches "CALENDAR" activity OUR WATCHFACE PORTFOLIO https://play.google.com/store/apps/dev?id=5591589606735981545 WEBSITE https://amoledwatchfaces.com
